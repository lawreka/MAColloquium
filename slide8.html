Anthropomorphism: conclusions
Even at rest, the proportions of the face can create perceptions of gender and have an emotional impact on the perception of the agent's face in terms of likability, intelligence, trustworthiness, and friendliness. Emotional animation should be limited to what is appropriate for the agent's task: complicating the interface with emotions that are unconvincing or repetitive will break the illusion of sentience and may make the interface less pleasant to use.
